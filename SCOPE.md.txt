# UK Grid Connection Deliverability Analyser

## Problem

Great Britain's electricity connections queue reached 701 GW (transmission + distribution) as of March 2024, with Ofgem warning it could reach 800 GW by end-2024. Some customers have been offered connection dates in the late 2030s.

NESO's connections reform has moved from "first-come" to "readiness + need", but visibility gaps remain.

## What I'm Building

A reproducible, versioned tool that:

- Pulls public queue and capacity data from NESO and DNO sources
- Scores deliverability transparently (rules + weights, explainable)
- Flags risks early (projects likely to stall or churn)
- Finds bottleneck patterns (by region, technology, TO/DNO area)
- Maps clustering opportunities (shared reinforcement candidates)

## What I'm NOT Building

- A regulatory decision tool
- A replacement for NESO's official queue management
- A commercial product
- Anything using private or scraped data

## Success Metrics

- Working data pipeline pulling live NESO data
- Transparent scoring model with documented logic
- Dashboard answering: "What changed? Why is this project ranked here?"
- Auto-generated weekly decision memo with evidence links

## Data Sources

- NESO TEC Register (CKAN API)
- NESO Embedded Register (CKAN API)
- GSP boundary GeoJSON
- SP Energy Networks capacity data (Opendatasoft)
